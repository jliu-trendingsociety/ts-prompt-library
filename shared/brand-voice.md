# Trending Society - Brand Voice & Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Mission:** Empower creators and businesses to build authentic social presence through AI-powered automation.

**Vision:** Make professional-grade content creation accessible to everyone, regardless of budget or technical skill.

**Values:**
- **Authenticity First:** AI should enhance, not replace, human creativity
- **Accessibility:** Enterprise-grade tools at startup prices
- **Transparency:** No black boxes - users understand what AI does
- **Results-Driven:** Measurable outcomes over vanity metrics

## Voice & Tone

### Core Voice Attributes

#### 1. **Knowledgeable but Not Condescending**
**✅ Do:**
- "Here's a better way to do X..."
- "Most teams struggle with Y. Here's how to fix it."
- "Let me show you a quick trick..."

**❌ Don't:**
- "You're probably doing this wrong..."
- "Everyone knows that..."
- "Obviously, you should..."

---

#### 2. **Confident but Not Arrogant**
**✅ Do:**
- "This works. Here's the data."
- "We've helped 500+ teams do this."
- "Our approach is different. Here's why."

**❌ Don't:**
- "We're the best in the industry."
- "No one else can do this."
- "You'd be crazy not to use us."

---

#### 3. **Friendly but Professional**
**✅ Do:**
- "Hey there! Let's dive in."
- "Quick question for you..."
- "Here's what I'd recommend..."

**❌ Don't:**
- "Yo! Check this out!"
- "OMG this is amazing!!!"
- "You won't BELIEVE what happened next..."

---

#### 4. **Data-Driven but Relatable**
**✅ Do:**
- "Teams save 20 hours/week on average."
- "Most users see 3x engagement in 30 days."
- "Here's what the data shows..."

**❌ Don't:**
- "Synergize your social ROI paradigm."
- "Leverage our platform to maximize KPIs."
- "Revolutionary, game-changing solution."

---

## Tone by Context

### Marketing Content (Ads, Landing Pages)
**Tone:** Bold, benefit-focused, urgent (but not pushy)

**Example:**
```
Stop Wasting 20 Hours/Week on Social Content

500+ marketing teams automated their workflow.
See how in 2 minutes.

[Watch Demo]
```

---

### Product UI (In-App Messages)
**Tone:** Helpful, encouraging, clear

**Example:**
```
✨ Nice work! Your first post is scheduled.

Want to set up auto-posting? It takes 2 minutes.

[Set Up Now] [Maybe Later]
```

---

### Support/Help Content
**Tone:** Patient, step-by-step, reassuring

**Example:**
```
Having trouble connecting your Instagram?

Here's what usually fixes it:
1. Make sure you're logged into Instagram
2. Click "Reconnect Account"
3. Grant permissions

Still stuck? Chat with us - we're here to help.
```

---

### Sales/Outreach
**Tone:** Consultative, ROI-focused, respectful of time

**Example:**
```
Hi Sarah,

I noticed you downloaded our SaaS content playbook.

Most SaaS teams in your situation are dealing with:
• Limited content resources
• Inconsistent posting
• Low engagement

Want to see how we're helping companies like ACME automate this?

15-min demo, no pitch. Just solutions.

Best,
Jeff
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Use Active Voice
**✅ Do:** "Our AI analyzes your audience"  
**❌ Don't:** "Your audience is analyzed by our AI"

### Use Specific Numbers
**✅ Do:** "Save 20 hours/week"  
**❌ Don't:** "Save lots of time"

### Use "You" Language
**✅ Do:** "You'll see results in 30 days"  
**❌ Don't:** "Users will see results"

### Avoid Jargon
**✅ Do:** "Automatically post to social media"  
**❌ Don't:** "Leverage our omnichannel distribution engine"

### Keep It Scannable
**✅ Do:**
```
Benefits:
• Save 20 hours/week
• 3x engagement
• AI-powered content
```

**❌ Don't:**
```
Our platform offers numerous benefits including significant time savings estimated at approximately 20 hours per week, enhanced engagement metrics showing approximately 3x improvement, and cutting-edge artificial intelligence-powered content generation capabilities.
```

## Power Words (Use These)

### Time/Speed
- Instantly, Minutes, Fast, Quick, Now, Today

### Simplicity
- Easy, Simple, Effortless, Automatic, Done-for-you

### Proof
- Proven, Verified, Guaranteed, Tested, Real

### Exclusivity
- Exclusive, Secret, Insider, Hidden, Limited

### Results
- Results, ROI, Growth, Increase, Boost, Transform

---

## Words to Avoid

### Overused Buzzwords
- ❌ Revolutionary
- ❌ Game-changing
- ❌ Disruptive
- ❌ Synergy
- ❌ Leverage (as verb)
- ❌ Paradigm
- ❌ Cutting-edge (unless actually new tech)

### Vague Claims
- ❌ Best
- ❌ Leading
- ❌ Top-rated (unless cited)
- ❌ World-class
- ❌ Premium (without context)

### Pushy Language
- ❌ Act now!
- ❌ Limited time only! (unless true)
- ❌ You're missing out!
- ❌ Don't be left behind!

## Segment-Specific Voice Adaptations

### For Frustrated Marketers
**Tone:** Empathetic, solution-focused  
**Language:** "Stop wasting...", "Finally...", "No more..."

**Example:**
> "Tired of spending hours on social posts that get buried in 2 minutes? Here's how 500+ marketers automated their workflow without losing quality."

---

### For Time-Poor Founders
**Tone:** Direct, no-fluff, time-conscious  
**Language:** "Quick...", "In minutes...", "One-time setup..."

**Example:**
> "You have 10 minutes? That's all it takes. Set it up once, runs forever. No team needed."

---

### For Data-Driven Users
**Tone:** Analytical, proof-heavy  
**Language:** "Data shows...", "Verified results...", "Benchmark..."

**Example:**
> "Companies like yours see 3x engagement increase on average (verified across 200+ teams). See the full benchmark report."

---

### For Enterprise Buyers
**Tone:** Authoritative, security-focused  
**Language:** "Enterprise-grade...", "SOC2 compliant...", "Fortune 500..."

**Example:**
> "Join IBM, Salesforce, and 50+ enterprise teams managing 10M+ posts/month on our platform. SOC2 certified, 99.9% uptime, dedicated support."

## Call-to-Action Patterns

### High-Commitment CTA
- "Book Enterprise Demo"
- "Talk to Sales"
- "Schedule Consultation"

### Medium-Commitment CTA
- "See How It Works"
- "Watch 2-Min Demo"
- "View Case Studies"

### Low-Commitment CTA
- "Start Free Trial"
- "No Credit Card Required"
- "Try It Free"

## Email Subject Line Formulas

### Curiosity
- "The [X] mistake costing you [Y hours/dollars]"
- "Why [Number] teams quit [Pain Point]"
- "What [Competitor] doesn't want you to know"

### Benefit
- "Save [X hours/dollars] with this [solution]"
- "[Number]x your [metric] in [timeframe]"
- "How to [achieve result] without [pain point]"

### Personalization
- "{{name}}, your [resource] is ready"
- "Quick question about {{company}}"
- "{{name}}, noticed you [action]"

### Urgency (Use Sparingly)
- "Spots filling up for [event]"
- "Last chance: [offer] ends [date]"
- "{{name}}, your trial expires in 3 days"

```
         (Slightly casual,
          professional)

Playful ←--------●-------------→ Serious
         (Occasionally playful,
          mostly serious)

Bold ←-----------------●-------→ Conservative
               (Bold in claims
                but data-backed)

Innovative ←----------●--------→ Traditional
              (Innovative solutions,
               traditional values)
```

## Content Checklist

Before publishing any content, verify:
- [ ] Uses "you" language (not "users" or "customers")
- [ ] Specific numbers (not "lots" or "many")
- [ ] Active voice (not passive)
- [ ] Benefit-focused (not feature-focused)
- [ ] Scannable (bullets, short paragraphs)
- [ ] No jargon or buzzwords
- [ ] Clear CTA
- [ ] Matches brand tone for context
- [ ] Free of typos and grammar errors
- [ ] Passes "read aloud" test (sounds natural)

## Examples of Brand Voice in Action

### Bad Example ❌
```
"Our revolutionary AI-powered platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to synergize your social media marketing efforts and maximize ROI through automated content distribution across omnichannel touchpoints."
```

**Why it's bad:**
- Buzzword soup
- Jargon-heavy
- No specific benefit
- Passive construction
- Not scannable

---

### Good Example ✅
```
"Stop spending 20 hours a week on social posts.

Our AI creates, schedules, and publishes your content automatically.

500+ teams are already saving time. See how in 2 minutes.

[Watch Demo]"
```

**Why it's good:**
- Clear benefit (save 20 hours)
- Specific action (creates, schedules, publishes)
- Social proof (500+ teams)
- Clear CTA
- Scannable
- Active voice
