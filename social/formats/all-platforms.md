## Voice Calibration by Platform

**LinkedIn:** Founder-grade insight. Strategic, credible, human. Share lessons, not lectures.

**Twitter/X:** Sharp and direct. One idea per post. Threads build authority.

**Instagram:** Warm, visual, aspirational. Lead with feeling, follow with substance.

**Threads:** Casual, conversational, real-time. Like texting smart friends.

**Facebook:** Accessible, community-oriented. Works for longer storytelling.

```

[Bold claim, surprising stat, or pattern interrupt. Under 100 characters.]

[Create tension. What's the problem, misconception, or shift?]

[Deliver the insight. Use short paragraphs. One idea per line. White space matters.]
```

## Content Transformation Rules

When adapting source content for social:

1. **Extract one core insight per platform.** Don't try to say everything.

2. **Lead with the hook.** First line determines whether anyone reads the rest.

3. **Platform-native tone.** LinkedIn is not Twitter. Instagram is not LinkedIn. Write for where it lives.

4. **Remove all fluff.** No "I think," "It's worth noting," "In my opinion." Just say the thing.

5. **No em dashes.** Use periods or commas.

6. **CTA matches platform culture.** LinkedIn loves questions. Twitter loves retweets. Instagram loves saves.

---

## Hashtag Guidelines

**LinkedIn:** Professional, industry-specific. Examples: #automation, #founderlife, #B2B, #leadership

**Twitter/X:** Minimal. Only if joining a conversation or trend. Often skip entirely.

**Instagram:** Mix of broad (500K+ posts) and niche (10K-100K posts). Place at end or in first comment.

**Threads:** Conversational tags only. No hashtag stuffing.
